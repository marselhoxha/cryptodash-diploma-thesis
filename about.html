Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about CryptoDash

Is CryptoDash free to use? ‚Üì

Yes, CryptoDash is completely free to use. We provide real-time cryptocurrency data, charts, news, and analysis tools at no cost. Our mission is to make crypto market information accessible to everyone.

How accurate is the data? ‚Üì

We source data from reputable APIs including CoinGecko, CryptoCompare, and Alternative.me. Our data is updated in real-time and cross-verified across multiple sources to ensure accuracy and reliability.

Can I use this for trading decisions? ‚Üì

CryptoDash provides educational and informational content only. While our data is accurate and comprehensive, it should not be considered financial advice. Always conduct your own research and consult professionals before making investment decisions.

Do you store personal data? ‚Üì

CryptoDash respects your privacy. We only store minimal data necessary for functionality (like theme preferences) locally in your browser. We don't collect personal information or require registration.

How often is data updated? ‚Üì

Price data and market statistics are updated every 30 seconds to provide near real-time information. News content is refreshed every 5 minutes, and chart data updates dynamically based on the selected timeframe.

Which cryptocurrencies are supported? ‚Üì

We support 100+ major cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin, Ethereum, and all top market cap coins. Our coverage includes detailed price data, charts, and market statistics for each supported asset.
